The shark is set up to seek the leader fish. 
Seek and Persue are enabled on the script in the inspector.

The leader fish is set up to flee from the shark. Flee and Path follow are enabled in the script in the inspector.

The rest of the fish are also set up to flee from the shark.Flee and Path follow are enabled in the script in the inspector.


If you disable flee from the leader fish and only have path enabled it will not flee from the shark. The shark can then eat the fish.
I also enable arrive on the shark so he can eat the fish.

The fish called Fish1 is set up to flee from the shark. Flee and Path follow are enabled in the script in the inspector.
